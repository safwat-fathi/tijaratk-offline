# Landing Page Creative & UI/UX Guide — تجارتك (Mobile First)

This guide is built specifically for the current positioning of تجارتك as a simple ordering and operations platform for Egyptian local merchants. It follows the branding direction and product philosophy from the shared reference document. 

The landing page should feel:

* Simple
* Trustworthy
* Fast
* Familiar to Egyptian merchants
* Non-technical
* WhatsApp-native
* Local and human

Most importantly:
The visitor should feel:

> "دي حاجة هتسهل شغلي فعلًا"

—not:

> "دي منصة تقنية معقدة"

---

# 1. Core Landing Page Goal

The landing page has ONE main job:

## Convert merchants from:

"أنا باخد الطلبات عادي على واتساب"

## To:

"أنا محتاج أجرب دي حالًا"

---

# 2. Target Audience Understanding

## Main Audience

* بقالة
* سوبر ماركت صغير
* محل خضار وفاكهة
* محل مواد غذائية
* أصحاب صفحات بيع محلية

## Their Reality

They already:

* Use WhatsApp all day
* Receive chaotic orders
* Forget orders
* Miss messages
* Repeat prices constantly
* Write orders manually
* Handle delivery manually

## They do NOT care about:

* SaaS
* AI
* Automation
* Dashboards
* Analytics
* APIs
* Technical words

## They care about:

* السرعة
* تقليل الغلطات
* تنظيم الطلبات
* شكل احترافي قدام العميل
* توفير وقت
* معرفة فلوس اليوم

---

# 3. Mobile-First Design Philosophy

This page MUST be designed for:

* One-hand usage
* Fast scrolling
* Thumb-friendly interactions
* Egyptian mobile internet conditions

## Priorities

1. Fast loading
2. Clear CTA
3. Big readable Arabic text
4. Very low cognitive load
5. WhatsApp familiarity

---

# 4. Overall Visual Direction

Based on the branding direction:

* Clean
* Soft
* Modern but not startup-techy
* Friendly
* Human
* Egyptian local-commerce vibe

Avoid:

* Futuristic visuals
* Corporate SaaS look
* Complex gradients
* Heavy dashboards
* Too much glassmorphism
* Tech jargon illustrations

---

# 5. Hero Section (MOST IMPORTANT)

## Goal

The merchant should understand the product in 5 seconds.

---

## Layout (Mobile)

### Top Navbar

Very small:

* Logo
* زر "ابدأ مجانًا"

Sticky after scroll.

---

## Hero Content Order

### 1. Headline

Must be extremely simple.

### Recommended Headline Options

#### Option 1 (Recommended)

# "نظّم طلباتك على واتساب بسهولة"

#### Option 2

# "خلي طلبات محلك أسهل وأسرع"

#### Option 3

# "طلبات أقل لخبطة. وشغل أهدى."

---

## 2. Supporting Text

Short.
Very conversational Egyptian Arabic.

### Example

"استقبل الطلبات بشكل منظم بدل رسايل الواتساب الكتير والمكالمات اللي بتتلخبط."

---

## 3. Primary CTA

VERY IMPORTANT.

### Button Copy

* "ابدأ مجانًا"
* "جرّب دلوقتي"
* "اعمل لينك الطلب بتاعك"

Avoid:

* سجل الآن
* إنشاء حساب
* ابدأ رحلتك الرقمية

---

## 4. Secondary Trust Text

Example:

* "بدون عمولة"
* "سهل لأي حد يستخدمه"
* "يشتغل من الموبايل"

---

## 5. Hero Visual

This is critical.

DO NOT show:

* Complex dashboards
* Analytics
* Charts

Instead show:

* A realistic Egyptian WhatsApp ordering experience
* Mobile mockup
* Clean order flow
* Arabic UI
* Simple order card

---

# 6. Hero Creative Direction

## Best Creative Concept

### "قبل / بعد"

#### Before

WhatsApp chaos:

* رسائل كتير
* صوتيات
* "هاتلي زيت كمان"
* missed messages

#### After

Simple organized order:

* اسم العميل
* المنتجات
* العنوان
* الحالة

This visual communicates instantly.

# 13. Pricing Section

Keep it SIMPLE.

DO NOT create startup pricing cards full of features.

---

## Better Structure

### Free Plan Card

"ابدأ مجانًا"

Includes:

* لينك طلبات
* استقبال الطلبات
* متابعة الطلبات

---

### Pro Plan Card

"لو عاوز تنظم شغلك أكتر"

Includes:

* تقارير
* تنظيم التوصيل
* ملخصات يومية
* أدوات للعملاء

---

## Pricing Tone

Avoid:

* Premium
* Enterprise
* Growth

Use:

* بسيط
* مناسب
* حسب احتياجك

# 17. Typography Usage

Based on the branding:

* Big bold headlines
* Comfortable spacing
* Large tap areas
* Arabic readability first

---

## Recommended Arabic Fonts

If not already defined in branding:

* Alexandria
* IBM Plex Sans Arabic
* Cairo

---

# 18. Spacing & Layout Rules

## Mobile Spacing

* Large vertical breathing room
* Short text blocks
* 16–20px side padding
* Cards with rounded corners

---

## Button Rules

Buttons must:

* Be large
* Sticky where important
* Thumb-friendly
* Full width on mobile

---

# 19. Animation Guidelines

Keep animations:

* Soft
* Minimal
* Useful

Good:

* Fade
* Slide
* Micro-interactions

Avoid:

* Heavy parallax
* Complex transitions
* Fancy WebGL effects

---

# 20. Landing Page Emotional Strategy

The page should emotionally communicate:

## Not:

"دي منصة تقنية"

## But:

"دي هتريحني في الشغل اليومي"

That is the core marketing angle.

# 22. Critical UX Notes

## DO:

* Keep content short
* Use colloquial Egyptian Arabic
* Use real examples
* Focus on outcomes

---

## DON'T:

* Explain technology
* Mention SaaS
* Mention AI heavily
* Use startup language
* Overwhelm users

---

# 23. Important Copywriting Direction

Every sentence should answer:

## "إيه اللي هيفيدني أنا كمحل؟"

Not:

## "إحنا عندنا features كتير"

# 25. Final Strategic Direction

The strongest positioning for تجارةك currently is NOT:

> "منصة تجارة إلكترونية"

The strongest positioning is:

> "طريقة أسهل لتنظيم طلبات المحل اليومية"

That positioning fits:

* Egyptian market behavior
* Merchant psychology
* Your MVP scope
* Your bootstrap constraints
* Your WhatsApp-first strategy

And it is highly aligned with the product philosophy in the reference document.
